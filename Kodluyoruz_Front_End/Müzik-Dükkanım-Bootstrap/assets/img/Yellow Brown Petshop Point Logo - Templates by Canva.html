Logo Templates by Canva

A good logo is essential for any brand or business. But with Canva’s free logo design templates, making one doesn’t have to be difficult or expensive. Canva’s logo templates are customizable, so you can edit them to your needs. You don’t need any design skills – just choose a layout you like and get started. There are thousands of templates and fonts to try, so you can find what best represents your business. There’s no need to hire a designer.

It’s important to think about what you want to communicate with your logo. Is your business fun and game-changing, or are you all about tradition and reliability? Different brand identities need different logos. The color you choose is important, too. Many businesses make their logo blue as this is the hue most associated with trustworthiness. Red is linked with hunger, which is why food brands like McDonalds opt for logos in this bold shade. Pink often conveys femininity and attitude. Green, meanwhile, has links with health, freshness and the environment.

The good news is Canva has plenty of free logo templates to choose from. Just have a look through our designs and see what fits for your business.Logo templates are royalty-free and won’t cost you anything to use. When you’re done, simply download your logo and off you go.
